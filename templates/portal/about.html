Qarshi davlat universiteti atrof-muhit barqarorligi siyosati

Universitet barqaror va ekologik mas'uliyatli akademik muhitni rivojlantirishga sodiqdir. Siyosat ta'lim, tadqiqot, boshqaruv va jamoatchilik bilan hamkorlik jarayonlariga barqarorlik tamoyillarini tizimli integratsiya qilishni nazarda tutadi.

  • tabiiy resurslardan oqilona foydalanish va ekologik mas'uliyatni kuchaytirish;
  • issiqxona gazlari chiqindilarini kamaytirish va energiya samaradorligini oshirish;
  • suv, chiqindi, transport va iste'mol boshqaruvida ilg'or yondashuvlarni joriy etish;
  • milliy va xalqaro ekologik me'yorlar hamda standartlarga muvofiqlikni ta'minlash.
{% else %}

Environmental sustainability policy of Karshi State University

The University treats environmental sustainability as a core institutional priority and integrates sustainability principles into academic, research, operational, and community-facing activities.

  • responsible use of natural resources and stronger environmental accountability;
  • reduction of greenhouse gas emissions and improved energy efficiency;
  • advanced approaches to water, waste, transport, and sustainable consumption;
  • alignment with national and international environmental standards.
{% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %} Barqaror xaridlar siyosati (2025-2030) {% else %} Sustainable procurement policy (2025-2030) {% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %}

Barqaror xaridlar siyosati universitet xarid faoliyatida iqtisodiy samaradorlik, ekologik mas'uliyat va ijtimoiy barqarorlik tamoyillarini joriy etishga qaratilgan.

  • narx, texnik sifat va ESG ko'rsatkichlari asosida tender baholash tizimi qo'llanadi;
  • energiya samarador uskunalar, ekologik sertifikatlangan mahsulotlar va kam chiqindili materiallarga ustuvorlik beriladi;
  • mahalliy ishlab chiqaruvchilar, kichik biznes va ijtimoiy mas'ul yetkazib beruvchilar rag'batlantiriladi;
  • 2030 yilgacha sustainable procurement share 40%, eco-certified products 30%, supplier ESG compliance 70% va energy efficient equipment 50%ga yetkazish ko'zda tutilgan.
{% else %}

The sustainable procurement policy introduces economic efficiency, environmental responsibility, and social sustainability into university purchasing decisions.

  • tenders are evaluated through price, technical quality, and ESG performance criteria;
  • priority is given to energy-efficient equipment, eco-certified goods, and lower-waste materials;
  • local producers, small businesses, and socially responsible suppliers are encouraged;
  • the 2030 targets include a 40% sustainable procurement share, 30% eco-certified products, 70% supplier ESG compliance, and 50% energy-efficient equipment.
{% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %} Barqaror investitsiya siyosati (2025-2030) {% else %} Sustainable investment policy (2025-2030) {% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %}

Barqaror investitsiya siyosati universitetning barcha investitsion qarorlariga ESG, yashil iqtisodiyot va uzoq muddatli iqtisodiy samaradorlik mezonlarini integratsiya qiladi.

  • kapital qo'yilmalar, infratuzilma loyihalari, xalqaro grantlar, modernizatsiya va PPP loyihalariga tatbiq etiladi;
  • har bir loyiha Environmental impact, Energy efficiency, Social impact, Governance va Financial sustainability mezonlari bo'yicha baholanadi;
  • minimal ESG ball 60 bo'lib, undan past loyihalar moliyalashtirilmaydi;
  • ko'mirga asoslangan energetika, tamaki, qurol ishlab chiqarish, jiddiy ekologik zarar yetkazuvchi va inson huquqlarini buzuvchi faoliyatlarga investitsiya kiritilmaydi;
  • moliyalashtirish manbalari davlat budjeti, maqsadli jamg'armalar, xalqaro moliya institutlari, yashil obligatsiyalar va xususiy investitsiyalarni o'z ichiga oladi.
{% else %}

The sustainable investment policy integrates ESG, green economy principles, and long-term financial viability into the University's investment decisions.

  • it applies to capital investments, infrastructure, grants, modernization projects, and public-private partnership initiatives;
  • each project is scored against environmental impact, energy efficiency, social impact, governance, and financial sustainability criteria;
  • the minimum ESG threshold is 60 points, and projects below that score are not financed;
  • the University excludes coal-based energy, tobacco, weapons production, severe environmental harm, and human-rights-violating activities from investment;
  • funding sources include the state budget, targeted funds, international finance institutions, green bonds, and private investment.
{% endif %}

{% if current_language == "uz" %}

SDG qo'mitasi universitetning barqarorlik bo'yicha strategik qarorlari, maqsadlar monitoringi va fakultetlararo muvofiqlashtirishini boshqaradigan asosiy institutsional platforma hisoblanadi.

  • rektorat, tegishli prorektorlar, bo'lim rahbarlari va texnik ijro guruhlarini birlashtiradi;
  • har chorakda ustuvor indikatorlar, loyiha ijrosi va xavf nuqtalarini ko'rib chiqadi;
  • SDGlar kesimida kampus, xarid, investitsiya va jamoatchilik aloqalarini uyg'unlashtiradi.
{% else %}

The SDG committee serves as the University's coordinating body for strategic sustainability decisions, target monitoring, and cross-unit alignment.

  • it brings together senior leadership, operational heads, and technical delivery teams;
  • it reviews quarterly indicators, implementation progress, and institutional risks;
  • it aligns campus, procurement, investment, and public-engagement activity across the SDG agenda.
{% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %} Korrupsiyaga qarshi siyosat {% else %} Anti-corruption {% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %}

Universitet halollik, shaffoflik va manfaatlar to'qnashuvining oldini olish tamoyillariga asoslangan nol toqat yondashuvini qo'llaydi.

  • xarid va investitsiya qarorlarida hujjatlashtirilgan ekspertiza va tekshiruv tartibi qo'llanadi;
  • anonim murojaatlar, ichki audit va ijro nazorati mexanizmlari doimiy faoliyat yuritadi;
  • rahbar va xodimlar uchun halollik, etik me'yorlar va manfaatlar to'qnashuvi bo'yicha muntazam treninglar o'tkaziladi.
{% else %}

The University applies a zero-tolerance approach grounded in integrity, transparency, and conflict-of-interest prevention.

  • procurement and investment decisions follow documented review and verification procedures;
  • anonymous reporting, internal audit, and implementation oversight mechanisms remain active;
  • leaders and staff receive regular training on ethics, integrity, and conflict-of-interest standards.
{% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %} Jamoatchilik bilan ishlash {% else %} Public engagement {% endif %}
{% if current_language == "uz" %}

Jamoatchilik bilan ishlash universitetning ochiq muloqot, hamkorlik va ijtimoiy hisobdorlik standartlarini amaliyotga aylantiradi.

Public engagement turns the University's standards for openness, collaboration, and social accountability into visible practice.

  • it sustains structured relationships with communities, schools, NGOs, and public institutions;
  • results are shared through the portal, public events, and formal announcements;
  • student and civil-society initiatives are integrated into institutional delivery where relevant.
{% endif %}

  • equal opportunity and safe working standards are upheld across academic and administrative environments;
  • support is reinforced through mentoring, leadership pathways, and protection mechanisms;
  • gender equality indicators are tracked as a formal part of institutional monitoring.
